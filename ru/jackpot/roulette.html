Part of the coins from the amount of bets in each round is added to the jackpot. Each bet has a certain chance of winning the jackpot. When the jackpot is won, 70% of the jackpot amount will be received by 1 player. 30% of the jackpot will be received by the other players in the round. The amount a player will receive from 30% of jackpot depends on the amount their bets.

Only players who bet for PLG Coin can win 70% of the jackpot. The remaining 30% is distributed to ALL players' bets.

Player can win jackpot only if the probability that any of his bets in the round will win is less than 80%. For example, if you bet on red and black at the same round, then the probability that one of the bets will win is 14/15 = 93.33%, in that case you can't win jackpot.
