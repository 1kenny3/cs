How it works?

PRO MODE - automatic requests for the purchase of skins at the price you are interested in.

Select the item you want to buy, set the price at which you want to buy and set the quantity you need, please note that the price cannot be less than the minimum price that our site has determined automatically. Click the green "START BUYING" button to activate the purchase request for this item. Now other users will display information that someone has made a request to purchase this item, when selling items at the request price, the buyer is assigned instantly and automatically.

You can pause the purchase request by clicking the red "STOP BUYING" button. To completely delete the request, you must first suspend the purchase - click the red "STOP BUYING" button, and then click on the cross in the upper right corner of the request block.

You can change order price only once in 30 minutes.
